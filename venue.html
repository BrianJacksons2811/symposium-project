Venue & Travel

Upington, the historic gateway to the Northern Cape — a blend of heritage, modern amenities, and agricultural excellence

Conference Venue

Upington Protea Hotel Marriott

The Upington Protea Hotel Marriott offers state-of-the-art conference facilities capable of accommodating up to 250 delegates, ensuring a comfortable and productive environment for all attendees. The hotel itself boasts 87 rooms, and lies 7 km (about 12 minutes) from Upington International Airport.

Upington offers modern infrastructure, a safe and accessible environment, and a rich cultural heritage — set in a region with strong agricultural value chains in grapes, raisins, pecans, wool, and livestock, and significant integration with renewable energy and water management.

Why the Northern Cape?

An Agricultural Powerhouse

South Africa's largest province is a cornerstone of national food security and agri-processing, with robust collaboration between government, academia, and industry.

  • Raisins SA: 84% of national production
  • Pecans SA: 50% of the national harvest processed locally
  • Orange River Cellars: a leading wine producer
  • Premium exports: Karoo Lamb, Rooibos Tea & SA Wool
  • Growth: agri-processing, renewable energy & water-efficient irrigation
Explore the Region

Tourism & Attractions

Delegates (and accompanying partners) are encouraged to explore the region's rich offerings:

  • Natural wonders: the iconic Orange River corridor, Augrabies Falls National Park, and the Kgalagadi Transfrontier National Park
  • Agri-tourism: private game reserves and wine, brandy & gin tasting at various cellars
  • Adventure: Orange River boat cruises and river rafting on the mighty Orange River

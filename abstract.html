Submission Guidelines

Requirements
  • Abstracts must be no longer than 300 words.
  • Include a suitable title.
  • Include a photo and contact details of the corresponding author.
  • Contributions should report on new or innovative developments and/or applications of existing techniques to inform professionals and stimulate discussion.
Submission & Review
  • Submit to Mr Senzo Masikane — senzo@saiae.co.za, subject line "Symposium Abstract", by 14 August 2026.
  • Abstracts are reviewed by the SAIAE National Council.
  • Authors notified of acceptance by 31 August 2026; preliminary programme released 11 September 2026.

An official abstract template is provided with the symposium announcements. Contact senzo@saiae.co.za to request the template.
